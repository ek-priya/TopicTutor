POLYCET

The Polytechnic Common Entrance Test (POLYCET) is a state-level exam conducted in Andhra Pradesh for admission to diploma courses in engineering, non-engineering, and technology at various polytechnics.

Kishore Vigyan Protsahan Yojana (KVPY)

a fellowship program in India that aims to identify and encourage students with aptitude for research in basic sciences. It is initiated and funded by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India. The program aims to motivate students to pursue research careers in science and ensure the development of skilled scientists for the country.

Geoscience Olympiad

(also known as the International Earth Science Olympiad or IESO) is an annual competition for secondary school students focused on Earth Sciences. It aims to promote interest in Earth Sciences, enhance education and learning, and encourage friendly relations among young learners from different countries.
